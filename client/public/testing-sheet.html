How to use this sheet
Step through section by section
1. Enter your name at the top — results are saved against it.

2. Work through each tab in order. The tab bar shows your pass/total count per section.

3. Click any test row to expand the step-by-step instructions for that scenario.

4. Set the status to Pass, Fail, or leave as Pending.

5. Add notes in the text box — include anything unexpected, error messages, or observations.

Results auto-save to your browser and to Supabase when you enter your name. Use Export CSV to share results.
