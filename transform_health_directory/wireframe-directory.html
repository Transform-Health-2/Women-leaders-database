Women Leaders in Digital Health Database

Transform Health is building a global database of women leaders in digital health to increase visibility, representation, and engagement in leadership, policy, and technical spaces. This is in efforts to take forward recommendations from the Transform Health policy brief "Establishing Gender Equitable Foundations for Digital Health Transformation to Advance Universal Health Coverage" launched in 2024 to mainstream gender in digital health through increasing the number of women in leadership and decision-making roles.

Information submitted through this form may be featured in a publicly accessible database. By consenting, you agree that your profile information including your name, role, organisation, biography, and relevant links may be publicly displayed. Your email address will not be publicly displayed.

Screen 3 — Bespoke branching form · open items

  • Entry branch: "Adding myself" vs "Nominating someone else" — second path collects a public profile link before proceeding to consent
  • Consent gate: "No" → polite end screen; "Yes" → unlocks all profile sections sequentially
  • Expertise tags: Max 3 selections enforced client-side; remaining tags grey out once limit reached
  • Geographic scope: "National" selection reveals a country dropdown — all other regions are multi-select checkboxes
  • Shareable URL: OG meta tags (title, description, image) auto-generated so WhatsApp / Twitter / LinkedIn previews render correctly
  • Auto-email trigger: Apps Script sends confirmation with "update profile" link + follow-up survey link on submit
  • Profile image: JPEG/PNG upload → stored in Google Drive · admin approves before it goes live
  • Bio word count: Real-time counter enforces 100–150 word soft cap with a warning, not a hard block
