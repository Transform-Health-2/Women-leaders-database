# Submit Your Profile to the Women Leaders Directory — Complete Guide

Welcome! This guide walks you through submitting your profile to the Transform Health Women Leaders Directory. The process takes about **10-15 minutes**.

---

## Before You Start

**Do you have:**
- [ ] Your email address
- [ ] Your current role / job title
- [ ] Your organization name
- [ ] A short bio (a few sentences about your work)
- [ ] Your expertise areas (2-3 areas you specialize in)
- [ ] A LinkedIn profile link (optional but recommended)
- [ ] A professional photo (optional but recommended)

**You'll also need to confirm:**
- [ ] You're comfortable being listed in a public directory
- [ ] The information you share is accurate
- [ ] You're a woman leader in digital health (required for this directory)

---

## The 6-Step Process

### Step 1: Choose Your Path

When you click **"Submit Profile,"** you'll see two options:

#### Option A: "I want to submit my own profile" (Recommended)
- **Who this is for:** You want to be in the directory and are ready to fill out your full profile
- **Time:** 15 minutes
- **What you control:** Everything — your bio, expertise, photo, links
- **Good for:** Active leaders with current roles and full professional information

#### Option B: "I want to nominate a leader"
- **Who this is for:** You want to recommend someone else (colleague, mentor, etc.)
- **Time:** 5 minutes
- **What you fill in:** Basic info about the person you're nominating
- **Good for:** Amplifying visibility of leaders in your network

**For the rest of this guide, we'll assume Option A (self-submit).** See [Nomination Guide](03-nominator-guide.md) for detailed instructions on nominating others.

---

### Step 2: Consent

> **What this asks:** "I confirm I'm comfortable being listed in a public directory and that the information I share is accurate."

**What to do:**
1. Read the consent statement carefully
2. Check the box to confirm
3. Click **Next**

This is a required step — we need your permission before your profile can be published.

**Important notes:**
- Your profile will be **public** — anyone on the internet can find it
- Your **personal email will NOT** be displayed publicly (only admins see it)
- You can **delete your profile anytime** using the magic link we'll email you

---

### Step 3: Basic Info

This is where you introduce yourself.

| Field | What to Enter | Tips |
|-------|---------------|------|
| **First Name*** | Your first name | Required |
| **Last Name*** | Your last name | Required |
| **Email*** | Your primary email | We'll use this to send you updates and your profile management link. Keep it accurate. |
| **Current Role*** | Your job title | e.g., "AI Research Lead," "Health Policy Director," "Founding Director" |
| **Organization*** | Where you work | e.g., "WHO," "MIT D-Lab," "Gates Foundation," "Independent Consultant" |

**Fields marked * are required.**

**Examples of good basic info:**
- ✓ Name: Amara Okonkwo
- ✓ Role: Head of Digital Health Transformation
- ✓ Organization: Ministry of Health, Nigeria

**After you fill this out, click Next →**

---

### Step 4: Professional Details

This is where you describe your expertise and experience.

#### Expertise (Required)
You can select **up to 5 expertise areas** from a predefined list:

- AI / Machine Learning
- Digital health
- Health financing
- Health information systems
- Health systems strengthening
- mHealth
- Digital health policy
- Digital health strategy
- Digital health advocacy
- Digital health innovation
- Digital health transformation
- Digital health philanthropy
- Research
- Telemedicine
- Health workforce
- Other (if none apply, you can add custom)

**How to select:**
1. Click the checkbox next to each area that applies to you
2. You can select multiple — choose 2-5 that best describe your work
3. If your expertise doesn't fit the list, click **"Other"** and type it in

**Examples:**
- *If you work on AI for health diagnosis:* Select "AI / Machine Learning" + "Digital health"
- *If you work on policy:* Select "Digital health policy" + "Health systems strengthening"
- *If you're a researcher:* Select "Research" + your specific focus area

#### Years of Experience (Optional)
- How many years have you been working in digital health or related fields?
- Enter a number (e.g., "8" or "15+")
- This helps leaders understand your seniority level

#### Your Bio (Required)
Write a **short paragraph (3-5 sentences)** about your work and impact.

**Good bio = Personal + Specific + Impact-focused**

❌ **Bad examples:**
- "I work in health."
- "I'm interested in digital health innovation."
- "I have experience in multiple areas."

✓ **Good examples:**
- "I lead the AI research team at XYZ, where we build machine learning models to improve maternal health outcomes in sub-Saharan Africa. Over 8 years, I've published 12 peer-reviewed papers and trained 40+ researchers. I'm passionate about making AI tools accessible to low-resource settings."
- "As Health Policy Director for ABC Foundation, I design digital health policies for 12 African countries. My focus is ensuring that health information systems serve both healthcare providers and patients equitably."
- "I founded TeleMed Solutions to bring telemedicine to rural India. In 3 years, we've connected 50,000+ patients with doctors in 8 languages."

**Why your bio matters:**
- It's often the **first thing** people read about you
- It shows **impact** and **experience**
- It helps people decide if they want to connect with you

**Tips for writing your bio:**
1. **Start with your role/title** — so people immediately understand who you are
2. **Add specifics** — numbers, impact, geography, scale
3. **Show personality** — use "I" statements, be conversational
4. **Keep it concise** — 4-5 sentences is plenty
5. **Highlight your unique angle** — what makes you different?
6. **Use keywords** — think of what you'd want people to search for to find you

**After you fill this out, click Next →**

---

### Step 5: Location & Links

#### Where do you work? (Geographical Scope)

**Primary Country*** (Required)
- Select the country where you're primarily based or where your main work happens
- Dropdown with all countries

**Geographical Scope*** (Required)
- Select the geographic reach of your work:
  - **Local** — You work within a single city or region
  - **National** — You work within one country
  - **Regional** — You work across 2-5 countries, or one continent (e.g., "Sub-Saharan Africa")
  - **Global** — Your work spans multiple continents

**Countries of Operation** (Optional)
- If you work across multiple countries, list them here
- You can select multiple countries
- **Example:** If you're based in Kenya but work across 8 East African countries, select all 8

#### Professional Links (Optional)
- **LinkedIn Profile URL** — Link to your LinkedIn profile
  - Format: `https://www.linkedin.com/in/yourprofile`
  - People will click this to learn more about you
- **Personal Website / Blog** — If you have a personal website, portfolio, or blog

**Tips:**
- Copy your LinkedIn URL from your browser's address bar (you're on your profile page)
- Make sure URLs start with `https://`
- If you don't have a LinkedIn, that's fine — skip this field
- Only include links that are **current and active** — we'll check them!

**After you fill this out, click Next →**

---

### Step 6: Photo & Final Review

#### Photo (Optional but Recommended)
- Upload a **professional headshot** (JPEG or PNG)
- A photo makes your profile 2-3x more discoverable
- **Best practices:**
  - ✓ Clear, professional photo (headshot)
  - ✓ Well-lit, in-focus
  - ✓ Smiling and approachable
  - ✓ 500x500px or larger (landscape works too)
  - ✗ Don't use a group photo
  - ✗ Don't use a photo from 10+ years ago
  - ✗ Don't use a logo or abstract image

#### Notable Achievements (Optional)
- Add 2-3 bullet points of notable achievements or projects
- Examples:
  - "Led AI research team that published 12 peer-reviewed papers"
  - "Trained 400+ health workers in digital tools across East Africa"
  - "Developed national digital health policy for [Country]"
  - "Founded telemedicine startup serving 50,000+ patients"

#### Review Before Submitting
Before you hit "Submit," review your profile one more time:

- [ ] Name and email are correct
- [ ] Role and organization are accurate
- [ ] Your bio is clear and compelling
- [ ] Expertise areas are relevant
- [ ] Country and geographic scope are accurate
- [ ] Links are correct and active
- [ ] Photo looks professional

---

### Step 7: Submit & What Happens Next

**Once you click "Submit":**

1. **Your submission goes to our admin team**
   - We review it within 5-7 business days

2. **You'll receive an email:**
   - If approved: Your profile is now LIVE in the directory! We'll send you a link to view it.
   - If rejected: We'll explain why and offer the chance to resubmit
   - If pending: We're still reviewing — check back soon

3. **You'll get a "magic link"**
   - This link lets you update or delete your profile **anytime** without needing a password
   - Save this link or keep the email — you'll need it if you want to make changes

4. **Your profile is now discoverable**
   - Women leaders in your network can search and find you
   - You can share your profile link with colleagues

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| ❌ Mistake | ✓ Better Approach |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Role too vague: "Manager" | Be specific: "Health IT Program Manager" |
| Bio too short: "I work in health." | Add detail: "I lead digital health strategy for [Organization] in [Region]" |
| No expertise selected | Pick at least 2-3 that apply |
| Outdated photo or no photo | Use recent, professional headshot |
| Typos in bio or name | Proofread before submitting |
| Wrong country selected | Double-check your primary work location |
| Broken LinkedIn link | Test your link before submitting |
| Organization name unclear | Use the full, official organization name |

## What Happens If Your Profile Is Rejected?

This is **not permanent**. If your profile is rejected, we'll send you an email explaining why:

### Common Rejection Reasons:

**1. Incomplete Information**
- Missing required fields (bio, expertise, role)
- **Fix:** Fill in all required fields and resubmit

**2. Expertise Doesn't Align with Digital Health**
- Your role is outside the scope of digital health
- **Fix:** If your work is related to digital health, let us know and resubmit

**3. Profile Looks Like a Duplicate**
- Someone else with a very similar profile already exists
- **Fix:** Contact us with clarification — we can merge or confirm it's a different person

**4. Spam or Suspicious Submission**
- Information looks fabricated or promotional
- **Fix:** Resubmit with verifiable information (e.g., your real LinkedIn)

**You can always resubmit** with corrections or reach out to ask questions. We want your profile in the directory if you genuinely fit the criteria!

## After Your Profile Is Approved

### You're in the Directory!

Now what?
- **Share your profile** — Copy the link and share with your network
- **Keep it updated** — If your role changes, use the magic link to update
- **Monitor your activity** — You can see how many people clicked your LinkedIn link
- **Delete anytime** — Use the magic link if you ever want to be removed

### Updating Your Profile

If your role, organization, or expertise changes:

1. Find your profile in the directory
2. Click **"Manage Your Profile"**
3. Click **"Request an Update"**
4. We'll send you the magic link (same one from approval)
5. Click the link to edit your profile directly
6. Submit — changes go live immediately (no re-review needed for most updates)

### Removing Yourself

If you want to be removed from the directory:

1. Find your profile
2. Click **"Manage Your Profile"**
3. Click **"Request Deletion"**
4. Confirm — your profile is permanently removed within 1-2 days

## Troubleshooting

### I saved a draft but can't find it
- **Where it is:** Your draft is saved in your browser's memory
- **To access:** Go back to Submit Profile — it should auto-load
- **If it's gone:** Your browser's cache was cleared, or you switched browsers/computers
- **To prevent:** Finish submitting in one sitting

### I submitted but don't see my profile in the directory
- **Normal!** Profiles only appear after admin review (5-7 days)
- **Check your email** for confirmation (might be in spam folder)
- **Contact us** if it's been over 7 days with no update

### My profile was rejected. Can I resubmit?
- **Yes!** Read the rejection reason in your email
- **Fix the issues** it mentions
- **Resubmit fresh** using the Submit Profile form
- **We'll review again** in 5-7 days

### I want to update my profile but lost the magic link email
- **Don't worry!** Go to your profile in the directory
- **Click "Manage Your Profile"**
- **Click "Request an Update"** — we'll send you a new link

### I submitted but never received the confirmation email
- **Check spam folder** (magic link emails sometimes get marked as spam)
- **Wait 24 hours** — sometimes emails take a bit to deliver
- **Contact us** with your email address, we can resend or verify your submission
