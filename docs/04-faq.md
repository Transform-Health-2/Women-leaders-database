# Transform Health Women Leaders Directory — Frequently Asked Questions

---

## For Everyone

### What is the Transform Health Women Leaders Directory?

A free, searchable global directory of women leaders working in digital health. Think of it as a discovery platform that increases visibility for women in technology, policy, leadership, and innovation roles across the health sector.

### Who can access it?

Anyone can search and view the directory for free—no login required.

### How is it different from LinkedIn?

LinkedIn is a personal professional network. This directory is **community-curated** and specifically focused on women leaders in digital health. It's:
- **Easier to discover:** Filtered specifically by expertise (AI, health financing, etc.) and geography
- **Verification-focused:** Profiles are reviewed by our admin team before publishing
- **Privacy-first:** You only share what you want public
- **Mission-driven:** Designed to increase representation in digital health

### Who maintains this directory?

The Transform Health Coalition team manages submissions, reviews profiles, and ensures data quality.

### Is my data safe?

Yes. We use Supabase (industry-standard encryption) and never sell data to third parties. Your personal email is stored privately and never displayed publicly. See our [Privacy Policy](06-privacy-policy.md) for details.

## For Women Leaders — Submitting & Managing Your Profile

### Should I submit a profile or get nominated?

**Self-submit if:**
- You want full control over your profile
- You want to write your own bio and select your expertise
- You're actively looking to expand your network and visibility

**Get nominated if:**
- Someone else (colleague, mentor, organization) wants to nominate you
- You prefer someone else make the initial submission
- A nomination creates a "lightweight" profile that you can expand later

Both paths lead to the same public directory. Either way, you can update or delete your profile anytime.

### I want to submit my profile. Where do I start?

1. Go to the Transform Health Directory homepage
2. Click **"Submit Profile"** button
3. Choose **"I want to submit my own profile"**
4. Follow the 6-step form:
   - **Step 1:** Consent (confirm you're comfortable being in the directory)
   - **Step 2:** Basic Info (name, email, role, organization)
   - **Step 3:** Profile Details (expertise, years of experience, bio)
   - **Step 4:** Links (LinkedIn, personal website, etc.)
   - **Step 5:** Photo (optional but recommended)
   - **Step 6:** Review & Submit

For detailed guidance, see [Submit Profile Step-by-Step Guide](02-submit-profile-guide.md).

### How long does it take for my profile to be reviewed?

We typically review submissions within **5-7 business days**. You'll receive an email confirmation when your profile is approved.

### What happens if my submission is rejected?

You'll receive an email explaining why. Common reasons:
- Incomplete information (missing required fields)
- Expertise doesn't align with digital health
- Role doesn't fit the "leader" criteria
- Duplicate or spam submission

You can resubmit with corrections, or contact us for clarification.

### How do I update my profile?

Two ways:

**Option A: From the directory**
1. Find your profile in the directory
2. Click the **"Manage Your Profile"** link
3. Click **"Request an Update"**
4. Describe what you want to change
5. Our admin team sends you a **magic link** (no password needed)
6. Click the link to edit your profile directly
7. Submit → we review → approved update goes live

**Option B: Direct edit link** (if you saved it)
- The magic link was emailed to you when your profile was first approved
- You can use that link anytime to update your profile

### What if I want to delete my profile?

1. Find your profile in the directory
2. Click **"Manage Your Profile"**
3. Click **"Request Deletion"**
4. Tell us why (optional)
5. We'll remove you from the directory within 5 business days
6. Your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered

### Can I update my profile myself, or does an admin review it?

For small updates (bio text, links), you update directly—no admin review needed.  
For significant changes, we may ask questions or request verification (e.g., if your role or expertise area changes dramatically).

### I never received my magic link email. What do I do?

Check your:
1. **Spam folder** (magic link emails can sometimes be marked as spam)
2. **Email address** — make sure you registered with the correct one
3. **Contact us** — we can resend the link if needed

### Can I submit multiple profiles (e.g., for different roles)?

No, the directory maintains one profile per person. If you have multiple roles, consolidate them into a single profile. For example:

**Instead of multiple profiles:**
- Profile 1: "AI researcher at XYZ University"
- Profile 2: "Board member at ABC Foundation"

**Use one profile:**
- Role: "AI Researcher & Policy Advisor"
- Bio: Mention both roles and expertise areas
- Organization: List your primary affiliation (update anytime)

### What does "Geographical Scope" mean?

It describes the geographic reach of your work:
- **Local:** Single city or region
- **National:** Single country
- **Regional:** 2–5 countries / one continent
- **Global:** Multiple continents

Select the option that best represents where you operate. You can also list specific countries you work in.

## For Directory Users — Searching & Viewing Profiles

### How do I search the directory?

**By Name/Keywords:**
1. Use the search box at the top
2. Type a name, role, organization, or keyword
3. Results update instantly

**By Expertise:**
1. Click the **"Expertise"** dropdown
2. Select one or more areas (AI, Telemedicine, Health Financing, etc.)
3. View all leaders with that expertise

**By Location:**
1. Click the **"Country"** or **"Continent"** dropdown
2. Select your region
3. View leaders operating in that area

**By Activity:**
1. Click the **"Sort by"** dropdown
2. Choose "Most LinkedIn Clicks" or "Least LinkedIn Clicks"
3. See which leaders are most actively engaged

You can combine filters. For example: "AI experts in Africa who were added in the last month."

### What information is shown for each profile?

**Public Info** (visible to everyone):
- Name, role, organization
- Photo
- Bio
- Expertise tags
- Years of experience
- Countries of operation
- LinkedIn or personal website links

**Private Info** (never shown):
- Personal email address
- Internal notes
- Submission timestamp

### I found a leader I want to connect with. What do I do?

Click on their profile to view their full details. Most profiles include:
- **LinkedIn link** — Click to visit their LinkedIn profile and send a message
- **Personal website** — Some leaders include their personal or organizational website

You can also use the information to search them externally (Google, Twitter, etc.).

### Can I download or export the directory data?

Not yet. You can search and view profiles in the directory, but bulk export functionality is not currently available. This is planned for a future version.

### Is there an API to query the directory?

Not yet, but we're planning one. Contact us if you're interested in integrations.

## For Nominators — Nominating a Leader

### Why would I nominate someone instead of letting them self-submit?

Good reasons to nominate:
- A leader in your network doesn't know about the directory
- You want to boost her visibility (great for recognition)
- She's busy and you want to get her started with basic info
- She's not on LinkedIn but you know her work

### How do I nominate a leader?

1. Go to the directory homepage
2. Click **"Submit Profile"**
3. Choose **"I want to nominate a leader"**
4. Fill in:
   - Her name (first & last)
   - Her role
   - Organization (optional but helpful)
   - Your name and email (so we can contact you if we have questions)
   - LinkedIn profile URL (optional)
   - Any additional context
5. Submit

### What happens after I nominate someone?

1. **Our admin team reviews** your nomination (3–5 days)
2. **Nominated profile goes live** with the basic info you provided
3. **Leader is notified** (optional) and can expand her profile anytime
4. You'll get an email confirmation

### Can I nominate someone who's already in the directory?

The system will warn you if there's a potential duplicate. Only nominate if you're confident it's a different person.

### What if the nomination is rejected?

Our admin team may reject if:
- The nominated person doesn't fit the "digital health leader" criteria
- The information provided is incomplete or unclear
- It's a spam or duplicate submission

You can resubmit with more information if needed.

## Troubleshooting

### The search isn't returning results I expect.

Try:
- **Simplify your search** — Use fewer filters at once
- **Check spelling** — Search is case-insensitive but spelling matters
- **Try partial names** — e.g., "Amara" instead of "Amarachi"
- **Expand your filters** — If searching by country + expertise, try one filter at a time
- **Reload the page** — Sometimes the search cache gets stuck

### A profile has outdated information.

Click on the profile and use the **"Report a Problem"** link to contact us. We'll investigate and update if needed.

### I found a duplicate or fake profile.

Please report it to us immediately. Click the profile and use the **"Report a Problem"** link. Include:
- The profile name and ID
- Why you think it's a duplicate
- Any relevant context

### My profile disappeared after I submitted it.

This can happen if:
1. **It was rejected** — Check your email for rejection reason. You can resubmit with corrections.
2. **It's still pending review** — Give it 7 business days. Profiles only show up after admin approval.
3. **It expired** — If you started a submission but didn't complete it, it may have been cleared. Start fresh.

### I'm having technical issues with the website.

Try:
- Clear browser cache (Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac)
- Use a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Disable browser extensions (they sometimes block features)
- Check that JavaScript is enabled

Still stuck? Contact us with:
- What you were trying to do
- What browser/device you're using
- Any error messages you see
