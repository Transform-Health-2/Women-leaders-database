# Privacy Policy & Data Handling Guide

**Last Updated:** June 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Introduction

The Transform Health Women Leaders Directory ("**the Directory**") is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your data. This policy explains:

- What data we collect
- Why we collect it
- How we use it
- How long we keep it
- Your rights and choices

---

## What Data We Collect

### When You View the Directory (Public Users)

If you simply **search and browse profiles**, we collect:
- IP address (for analytics only)
- General location (country/region, based on IP)
- Browser type and device type
- Pages visited and time spent
- Search queries and filters used

**We do NOT collect** identifying information unless you voluntarily provide it.

### When You Submit a Profile (Leaders)

If you **submit your own profile**, we collect:

#### Public Data (Displayed in Directory)
- First name, last name
- Professional photo (optional)
- Current role / job title
- Organization name
- Professional bio
- Expertise areas
- Years of experience
- Primary country and countries of operation
- Geographical scope of work
- LinkedIn profile URL (optional)
- Personal website / blog URL (optional)

#### Private Data (NOT Displayed, Admin Only)
- Personal email address
- Timestamp of submission
- Internal admin notes (e.g., "verify LinkedIn profile")
- Submission status (pending, approved, rejected)
- IP address of submitter

### When You Nominate Someone (Nominators)

If you **nominate a leader**, we collect:

#### About the Nominated Person
- First name, last name
- Professional role
- Organization (optional)
- LinkedIn or web profile URL (optional)
- Any additional context you provide

#### About You (the Nominator)
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your organization/relationship (optional)

### When You Request a Profile Update or Deletion (Leaders)

If you request an **update or deletion**, we collect:
- Your email address
- Reason for update/deletion (optional)
- Any changes you request

## How We Use Your Data

### For Leaders (Profile Submitters)

Your data is used to:
1. **Display your profile** in the public directory
2. **Process your submission** (review, approve/reject, contact you)
3. **Send you updates** — e.g., profile approved, comment on your submission
4. **Allow you to manage your profile** — update details, delete profile (via magic link)
5. **Track engagement** — count LinkedIn clicks to your profile (for analytics)
6. **Improve the directory** — aggregate statistics (e.g., "50% of leaders have 10+ years experience")

### For Public Users (Directory Searchers)

Your data is used to:
1. **Provide search & discovery functionality**
2. **Improve search relevance** (which searches are popular, which profiles are clicked)
3. **Generate analytics** (total searches, popular expertise areas, etc.)

### For Nominators

Your data is used to:
1. **Process the nomination** (verify details, contact you if we have questions)
2. **Contact you** if clarification is needed
3. **Verify the nomination isn't spam**

## Data Retention (How Long We Keep Your Data)

### Active Profiles
- **Duration:** As long as your profile is "live" in the directory
- **Your control:** You can delete anytime via magic link email

### Rejected/Pending Profiles
- **Duration:** 90 days after submission
- **After 90 days:** Deleted unless you resubmit within the window

### Deleted Profiles
- **Immediately removed from public directory**
- **Database record:** Retained in backups for 30 days (in case of accidental deletion)
- **After 30 days:** Permanently deleted
- **Exception:** If you have a pending update request, we retain data until resolved

### Email Addresses (For Update/Delete Requests)
- **Duration:** Until request is resolved
- **Then:** Deleted immediately

### Nomination Records
- **If approved:** Becomes a live profile → follow "Active Profiles" retention
- **If rejected:** Deleted after 90 days

### Analytics & Logs
- **Duration:** 90 days
- **Includes:** IP addresses, search queries, page views
- **Not connected to names** (anonymized)
- **After 90 days:** Purged

## Your Data Rights

Depending on where you're located, you have rights to:

### Right of Access
- **What:** Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **How:** Email us with "Data access request" in subject line
- **Timeline:** We'll respond within 30 days

### Right of Correction
- **What:** Correct inaccurate information
- **How:** Use the update profile link, or email us
- **Timeline:** Updates go live within 1-2 business days

### Right of Deletion ("Right to Be Forgotten")
- **What:** Request permanent deletion of your profile and data
- **How:** Use the delete profile link, or email us
- **Timeline:** Deleted within 1-2 business days (except backups, deleted after 30 days)

### Right to Data Portability
- **What:** Request your data in a common format (CSV, JSON)
- **How:** Email us with "Data export request"
- **Timeline:** We'll respond within 15 days

### Right to Withdraw Consent
- **What:** Withdraw consent to display your profile
- **How:** Click delete profile link, or email us
- **Effect:** Your profile is removed immediately

### Right to Opt Out of Analytics
- **What:** Opt out of being included in anonymized analytics
- **How:** Email us
- **Timeline:** We'll add you to our opt-out list within 24 hours

## Security & Data Protection

### How We Protect Your Data
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connection)
- **Encryption at rest:** Personal data encrypted in Supabase
- **Access control:** Only admin team can see private data (email, admin notes)
- **Password security:** Admin passwords are hashed and salted
- **Regular backups:** Daily backups stored securely
- **Security audits:** Regular vulnerability scans and penetration tests

### What We Do NOT Do
- Store passwords in plain text
- Share API keys or credentials
- Log sensitive information (emails, payment data)
- Expose data in public repositories or logs

### What You Should Do
- **Never share your magic link** (it's like a password)
- **Never post your private email** in your bio
- **Report suspicious activity** to us immediately

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)** | Data that identifies you (name, email, IP address) |
| **Processing** | Any operation on data (collect, store, use, delete) |
| **Data Subject** | The person whose data we hold (you) |
| **Data Controller** | The organization deciding how data is used (Transform Health) |
| **Data Processor** | The organization processing data on our behalf (Supabase) |
| **Retention** | How long we keep data |
| **Anonymized Data** | Data stripped of identifying information, cannot be linked back to you |
